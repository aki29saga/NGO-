Our Story

The Mabel Ihegboro Foundation was established in 2010 with a vision to create sustainable change in underserved communities across Nigeria. Founded by Dr. Mabel Ihegboro, a passionate advocate for education and community development, our foundation has grown from humble beginnings to become a beacon of hope for thousands.

What started as a small initiative to support school children in Lagos has expanded into a comprehensive organization addressing education, healthcare, and economic empowerment across multiple states.

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged individuals and communities by providing access to quality education, healthcare services, and economic opportunities, while fostering sustainable development and inspiring hope for a brighter future.

Our Vision

A Nigeria where every individual, regardless of their background, has equal access to opportunities that enable them to reach their full potential and contribute meaningfully to society.
