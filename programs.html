Education

Education Empowerment Initiative

Our flagship program provides scholarships, school supplies, and infrastructure support to underserved schools across Nigeria. Since 2012, we've awarded over 500 scholarships and renovated 15 schools.

  • Full scholarships for primary to university education
  • School renovation and construction projects
  • Teacher training workshops

Community Health Outreach

Our mobile clinics and health education programs bring essential medical services to rural communities. In 2022 alone, we provided free medical care to over 3,000 beneficiaries.

  • Maternal and child health programs
  • Disease prevention education
